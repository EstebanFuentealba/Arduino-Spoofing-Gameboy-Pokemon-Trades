Arduino-Spoofing-Gameboy-Pokemon-Trades
=======================================

A first gen Pokemon (Yellow, Blue, Red) trade spoofer that allows him to obtain pokemon without having two Game Boys only with Arduino

Pinout
--------------------------------------
Old "8bit" Plug:
```
 ___________
|  6  4  2  |
 \_5__3__1_/   (at cable)
 ```
| Pin | Name | Cable
|--------|--------|
|   1     |   VDD35     | N/A
|   2     |   SO     | Red
|   3     |   SI     | Orange
|   4     |   SD     | Brown
|   5     |   SC     | Green
|   6     |   GND     | Blue
